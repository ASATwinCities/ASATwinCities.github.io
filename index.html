Mission

The Twin Cities Chapter of the American Statistical Association aims to support and promote the field of Statistics in the Minneapolis-St. Paul metropolitan area and surrounding region by connecting academic and industrial statisticians both to each other and to national ASA initiatives.

Membership

  • We have a diverse membership group including statisticians, data scientists, and quantitative professionals from academia, government, and private industry.
  • Become a member now!

Events

  • We typically host spring and fall events each year with other ad-hoc events throughout the year.

ASA TC Leadership

We are happy to serve on behalf of the statistical community in the Twin Cities. Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer are elected by the ASA TC membership. Vice President will become President and President will become Past President each year. All other positions are appointed by the leadership committee.
